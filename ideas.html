Loop holes of education system in India and its possible solutions

Today many schools including govt. and private lack some basic infrastructure facilities like libraries, playgrounds ,science labs,etc. This can lead to lack of their personal growth. Therefore these basic necessities should be included in infrastructure in schools.

Quality education is must for all the students. Teachers should be weel trained. Students should also get practical knowledge along with theoritical. They should also be taught to solve real life problems. Industial visits and nature camps must be included in curriculum. Also the subject for their personal development should be included like finance, reasoning and aptitude.

Technological tools like AV rooms can be helpful for better understandings and enhancement of grasping power. Many schools inIndia lack this due to less fundings from government.

Fees for medical education is much higher than that of other branches. Also there are schools with low fees but lacks other facilities. These should be overcomed by providing scholarships to needful students. Can be made affordable by digitalization also. Books should be provided at low cost to needy students.
